Account Reinstatement

  • Reinstatement of a suspended account is the process to get the seller's account back up and running.
  • If you have a suspended seller account, & you want to get reinstatement and start selling again!
  • You are in the right place, I will give you the best chance at reinstating your account with the correct customized suspension appeal letter and plan of action (POA) for your situation.

Main reasons for an Account suspension:-

  • Multiple accounts held by the same person
  • Product listing is reported as a fake or counterfeit product
  • Product listing violates Amazon's Marketplace Items Condition Guidelines. e.g. used items which are sold as new
  • Safety complaints from customers
  • High negative Customer Experience Rating (NCX)
  • Expired items
  • Selling restricted/prohibited Items
  • Order Defect Rate (ODR) is too high
  • Other policy violations

5 Ways to Prevent Suspension or Reinstatement

Have the Correct Documentation

Before you open your seller account, make sure you have all the necessary paperwork. Any discrepancies in your paperwork can result in a denial of your application or a suspension in the future.

Remove Any Problematic Products

Make a risk assessment and value judgment before you decide that you couldn’t possibly consider deleting a favorite product. The busy season is not the time to be taking these risks, as it could come at a very costly price to your business after an already difficult year.

Double-Check Your Keywords

It’s quite common for sellers to go overboard when they list their products by overloading on the keywords they choose. This is costly for your Pay Per Click (PPC) campaigns.

Outsource The Tasks You can’t Handle

With the global increase in Sellers, the competition on Amazon's platform is increasing. Thankfully, this is something you can anticipate. Employ the help of specialists in the areas that are most important to you, or to those where you lack the knowledge.

Be Proactive And Honest

Stay up-to-date with all policy changes. Be aware of what people are saying about your business and the reviews they are leaving, and be present on your Account. The best way to stay on top of things is to remain attentive and know exactly what is happening on a daily basis.

Monitor Your Account On Daily Basis

Avoid an Account suspended situation by monitoring health of your account which means there should be no complaints about order defects, very few or no pre-fulfilment cancellation by seller. Monitor your listings for price and description at all times to ensure the prices match similar products offered by other sellers.
